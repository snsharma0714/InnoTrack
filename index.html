About InnoTrack

InnoTrack is a comprehensive portal designed to foster a culture of continuous improvement by tracking, measuring, and showcasing innovation excellence in educational institutes. Our platform aggregates data from various sources, providing insightful analytics, and presenting key innovation indicators in an intuitive and visually appealing manner.

We aim to encourage participation, facilitate data-driven decision-making, and highlight your institution's innovative achievements.
